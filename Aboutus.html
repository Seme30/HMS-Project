Haile Resorts And Hotels

Arba Minch is the home of two Ethiopia’s largest rift valley lakes (Abaya and Chamo), beautiful landscape (God’s Bridge), Forty (40) springs Nechisar National Park, Crocodile ranches, Crocodiles market and the highland of Dorze village on its doorstep. The city is also used as a gateway to the Omo valley and Konso on the Southern circuit. It is one of the best destinations in Ethiopia. Haile-Arba Minch is a 107 room four-star resort located at a grand view overlooking the twin lakes of Abaya and Chamo along the beautiful evergreen vegetation and chain of mountains that form the most beautiful pattern. Haile-Arba Minch is an all-inclusive concept that provides five-star services to its guests. Our new resort features food and beverage outlets on viewpoints; complete health club and wellness center, conference and wedding venue, and outdoor activities.
