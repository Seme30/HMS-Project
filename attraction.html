Reason to Visit Haile Resort Arbaminch

NECH SAR NATIONAL PARK


Nech Sar National Park includes a dense swathe of groundwater forest bordering the town of Arba Minch, the twin lakes Chamo and Abaya, the spiky acacia scrub of the mountainous bridge of God that divides them and the wide-open NechSar (‘White Grass’) plains.

Nech Sar National Park

CROCODILE MARKET


A popular excursion is a boat trip to Lake Chamo’s so-called crocodile market, a stretch of reed-lined sand flats populated by some truly gigantic Nile crocodiles as well as few pods of hippos and wealth of aquatic birds.

SOME OF OTHER ATTRACTIONS

Arba Minch Forest Abaya Lake Chamo Lake Arba minch (forty springs) Arba minch also serves as a hub to reach other destinations in the region for travellers using both road and air transport. Above all people are friendly and welcoming.
